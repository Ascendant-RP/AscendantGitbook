# Combat & NLR

## RDM (Random Deathmatch)

Killing or attempting to kill another player **without valid IC reason or proper RP buildup**.

❌ Pulling up to a stranger and shooting them.
❌ Spraying a crowd because you're losing a fight.
✅ Shooting someone who pulled a gun on you first.
✅ Eliminating a witness *after* RP buildup and a clear motive.

**You must initiate** before engaging in lethal force. Initiation = a clear demand or threat the other player can hear/read (voice or `/me`).

## VDM (Vehicular Deathmatch)

Using a vehicle as a weapon without RP justification.

❌ Running someone over because they cut you off.
❌ Ramming PD vehicles for fun.
✅ Hitting a fleeing suspect with your car as a cop, after attempts to stop them.

## NLR — New Life Rule

When your character dies and is revived (respawns at hospital):
- You **forget the events leading up to your death**, including who killed you and why.
- You **cannot return to the area of death for 15 minutes**.
- You **cannot seek revenge** on the person/group who killed you for that interaction.

You **do** remember:
- Long-term grudges established before the situation.
- Your character's general life, friends, job, etc.

## Combat Logging

Disconnecting during or immediately after a hostile situation = **combat log**. Bannable. If you crash, **rejoin immediately** and explain in `/ooc`.

## Mass RDM / Mass VDM

Killing **3 or more players** in rapid succession without RP = **permanent ban**, no warnings.

## Green Zones

The following are **No-Combat Zones**:
- Hospitals (Pillbox, Sandy, Paleto)
- Police Departments (interior + immediate exterior)
- Vehicle dealerships
- Spawn points

You may not initiate, shoot, rob, or stab anyone in these zones. Ongoing chases that **enter** a green zone are OK to continue, but new aggression is not.

## Hostage Situations

- Hostages must be physically restrained (`/me cuffs`, zip ties).
- Police negotiate. Don't execute hostages just because PD doesn't comply instantly — give it real time.
- Demands must be reasonable (an Oppressor MK2 and a billion dollars is not).

## Gang Conflict

- Gang vs gang shootouts require **prior RP**, not random street ambushes.
- See the [Factions Guide](../player-guide/factions.md) for conflict approval rules.
