# ⚔️ Combat & NLR

---

## RDM — Random Deathmatch

Killing or attempting to kill another player **without valid IC reason or proper RP buildup**.

| ❌ RDM | ✅ Not RDM |
|---|---|
| Pulling up to a stranger and shooting them | Shooting someone who pulled a gun on you first |
| Spraying a crowd because you're losing a fight | Eliminating a witness after RP buildup with a clear motive |
| Drive-by on someone for bumping into you | Retaliating against someone who attacked you in a prior scene |

{% hint style="warning" %}
**You must initiate before engaging in lethal force.** Initiation = a clear demand or threat the other player can hear or read (voice or `/me`).
{% endhint %}

---

## VDM — Vehicular Deathmatch

Using a vehicle as a weapon without RP justification.

| ❌ VDM | ✅ Not VDM |
|---|---|
| Running someone over because they cut you off | Hitting a fleeing suspect as a cop, after attempts to stop them |
| Ramming PD vehicles for fun | Pushing a blockade as part of an approved heist escape RP |

---

## NLR — New Life Rule

When your character is revived at the hospital after death:

**You forget:**
- The events directly leading up to your death
- Who killed you and why, for that scene
- Any information gathered during the situation

**You remember:**
- Long-term grudges and relationships established before the situation
- Your character's general life, job, friends, and goals

{% hint style="danger" %}
**You cannot return to the area of your death for 15 minutes.** You cannot seek revenge on the person or group who killed you in that interaction.
{% endhint %}

---

## Combat Logging

Disconnecting **during or immediately after** a hostile situation = combat log. This is a bannable offense.

{% hint style="info" %}
If you crash mid-combat, **rejoin immediately** and explain in `/ooc`. Honest mistakes are understood; deliberately logging is not.
{% endhint %}

---

## Mass RDM / Mass VDM

Killing **3 or more players** in rapid succession without valid RP = **permanent ban, no warnings, no appeal**.

---

## Green Zones (No-Combat Zones)

The following areas are **safe zones**. No initiating, shooting, robbing, or stabbing anyone in these locations:

- 🏥 All hospitals (Pillbox Medical, Sandy Shores, Paleto Bay)
- 🚔 Police Department interiors and immediate exterior
- 🚗 Vehicle dealerships
- 🏠 Character spawn points

{% hint style="warning" %}
Ongoing chases that **enter** a green zone may continue, but **new aggression cannot be started** inside one.
{% endhint %}

---

## Hostage Situations

- Hostages must be physically restrained (`/me` cuffs, zip ties, etc.).
- Police will negotiate. Do not execute hostages immediately just because PD doesn't comply instantly — give it realistic time.
- Demands must be reasonable. Demanding a private jet and $10 million will not be entertained.

---

## Gang Conflict

- Gang vs. gang shootouts require **prior IC RP buildup**, not random street ambushes.
- Sustained wars require OOC agreement and staff oversight — see [Factions & Gangs](../player-guide/factions.md).
