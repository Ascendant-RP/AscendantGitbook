# Punishment System

We try to be fair. Most rule breaks get a warning first — but severity, intent, and history all matter.

## Punishment Tiers

| Tier | Action | Typical Cause |
|---|---|---|
| **1 — Verbal** | Staff DMs you / talks IC. | Minor FailRP, first-time confusion. |
| **2 — Warning** | Logged in your record. | Minor RDM, light powergaming, repeated FailRP. |
| **3 — Kick** | Removed from server, can rejoin. | Toxicity, ignoring staff, repeated tier 2. |
| **4 — Temp Ban** | 1–14 days. | RDM, VDM, metagaming, NLR break. |
| **5 — Long Ban** | 14–90 days. | Repeat offenses, mass RDM, exploiting bugs. |
| **6 — Permanent Ban** | Removed from community. | Cheating, hate speech, doxxing, RMT, mass RDM. |

## Appeals

- Appeal in `#ban-appeals` on [Discord]([DISCORD_INVITE_URL]).
- Use the template. Be honest — we have logs.
- Appeals are reviewed within **5–7 days**.
- Appealing in bad faith (lying, ban evading) extends or makes the ban permanent.

## Ban Evasion

Creating a new account to bypass a ban = **permanent ban on all linked accounts**, including IP, hardware ID, and Discord. We log everything.

## Staff Discretion

Staff can apply punishments outside this table when situations require it. All major actions require **two-staff sign-off** above tier 4.

## Your Record

- Punishments stay on your account permanently for staff visibility.
- However, after **6 months of clean play**, prior tier 1–3 actions are not counted toward escalation.
