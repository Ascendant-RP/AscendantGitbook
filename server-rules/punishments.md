# ⚖️ Punishment System

We aim to be fair and consistent. Most rule breaks receive a warning first — but severity, intent, and history all factor into every decision.

---

## Punishment Tiers

| Tier | Action | Typical Cause |
|---|---|---|
| **1 — Verbal Warning** | Staff speaks to you in DMs or IC. Not logged formally. | Minor FailRP, first-time confusion about a rule |
| **2 — Warning** | Logged on your account record. | Minor RDM, light powergaming, repeated FailRP |
| **3 — Kick** | Removed from server, you can rejoin immediately. | Toxicity, ignoring staff, repeated tier 2 offenses |
| **4 — Temp Ban** | 1–14 days, depending on severity. | RDM, VDM, metagaming, NLR violations |
| **5 — Long Ban** | 14–90 days. | Repeated offenses, mass RDM, exploiting bugs |
| **6 — Permanent Ban** | Removed from the community entirely. | Cheating, hate speech, doxxing, RMT, mass RDM/VDM |

{% hint style="info" %}
Tier 4+ punishments require **two-staff sign-off** before being applied. No single staff member can temp-ban or perm-ban alone.
{% endhint %}

---

## Appealing a Ban

{% hint style="warning" %}
Appeal only through official channels. DMing staff directly to appeal is not allowed and will not speed up the process.
{% endhint %}

1. Go to `#ban-appeals` in our [Discord](https://discord.gg/ascendantrp).
2. Use the provided template — fill it out completely and honestly.
3. **We have logs.** Lying in your appeal makes things worse.
4. Appeals are reviewed within **5–7 days**.

---

## Ban Evasion

{% hint style="danger" %}
Creating a new account to bypass a ban results in a **permanent ban on all linked accounts**, including IP, hardware ID, and Discord. We cross-reference everything.
{% endhint %}

---

## Staff Discretion

Staff can apply punishments outside this tier table when situations call for it. All tier 4+ actions require a second staff member's sign-off. Unusual decisions are reviewed by Head Admin.

---

## Your Record

- All punishments are logged on your account permanently for staff visibility.
- After **6 months of clean, active play**, prior Tier 1–3 actions are flagged as inactive and will not count toward escalation.
