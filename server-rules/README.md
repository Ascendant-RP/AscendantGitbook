# 📜 Server Rules

These rules apply to **everyone** — players and staff alike. Ignorance of the rules is **not** an excuse. By connecting to Ascendant RP you agree to follow them at all times.

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Read before you play.** Rule enforcement is consistent and logged. "I didn't know" will not get your ban lifted.
{% endhint %}

---

## The Three Core Principles

If you remember nothing else, remember these:

> **1. Roleplay everything.** No "lol" in `/me`. No GTA-mode driving. Take your character seriously.

> **2. Value your life.** Don't do things your character would never do just because you "have nothing to lose."

> **3. Keep IC and OOC separate.** Drama that happens in character stays in character. Don't take losses personally.
