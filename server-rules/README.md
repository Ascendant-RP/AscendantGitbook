# Server Rules

These rules apply to **everyone** — players and staff alike. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. By connecting to Ascendant RP you agree to follow them.

## The Three Big Ones

If you remember nothing else, remember these:

1. **Roleplay everything.** No "lol" in /me. No GTA-mode driving. Take your character seriously.
2. **Value your life.** Don't do things your character would never do because you "have nothing to lose."
3. **Keep IC and OOC separate.** Drama in character stays in character. Don't take L's personally.
