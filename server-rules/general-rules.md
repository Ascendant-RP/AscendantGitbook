# 📌 General Rules

These are the community-wide rules that apply everywhere — in-game, in Discord, and in any Ascendant space.

---

## 1. Respect

- Treat every player and staff member with respect OOC (out of character).
- Zero tolerance for racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or any hate speech — IC or OOC.
- IC conflict is part of RP and is encouraged. OOC harassment is a **permanent ban**, no warnings.

---

## 2. No Cheating or Exploiting

{% hint style="danger" %}
Using cheats, mod menus, or exploiting bugs is an **immediate permanent ban** with no appeal window.
{% endhint %}

- No mod menus, injectors, scripts, or third-party tools that affect gameplay.
- No exploiting bugs for money, items, or in-game advantage. **Report bugs** — don't farm them.
- No stream-sniping or using external comms (Discord DMs, party chat) to gain IC information.

---

## 3. No Advertising

- Don't advertise other servers, communities, or services in any Ascendant channel or in-game.
- Actively recruiting players for other servers = **permanent ban**.

---

## 4. Voice & Mic Quality

- You must have a **working, clear microphone**. This is a voice-RP server.
- No voice changers, soundboards, or playing music over your mic.
- Speak **English** in main RP scenarios. Other languages are fine for designed character concepts approved in advance.

---

## 5. Streaming & Content Creation

- You're welcome to stream and record on Ascendant.
- Don't let your overlay dox other players (real names, IPs, etc.).
- Don't use information from someone else's stream to gain IC advantage — that's **metagaming**.
- Tag your content with **#AscendantRP** so we can find and share it.

---

## 6. Account Sharing

{% hint style="warning" %}
One Cfx.re account per person. Sharing accounts results in a ban for **both** accounts.
{% endhint %}

- One account per player — no exceptions.
- Don't let friends, family, or partners play on your character or account.

---

## 7. Real-Money Trading (RMT)

- You may **not** sell characters, items, in-game money, or accounts for real money.
- All monetary support must go through our [official store](https://ascendant-rp.tebex.io) only.
- RMT = **permanent ban** on all linked accounts.

---

## 8. Common Sense

{% hint style="info" %}
If you have to ask yourself "is this against the rules?" — it probably is. Ask a staff member first instead of testing it.
{% endhint %}
