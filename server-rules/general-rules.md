# General Rules

## 1. Respect

- Treat every player and staff member with respect OOC.
- No racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or any form of hate speech — IC or OOC.
- IC conflict is encouraged. OOC harassment is a permanent ban.

## 2. No Cheating or Exploiting

- No mod menus, injectors, scripts, or third-party tools that affect gameplay.
- No exploiting bugs for money, items, or advantage. **Report bugs**, don't farm them.
- No stream-sniping or use of external comms (Discord DMs, party chat) to gain IC info.

## 3. No Advertising

- Don't advertise other servers, communities, or services in chat, voice, or DMs.
- Recruiting players for other servers = permanent ban.

## 4. Voice & Mic Quality

- You must have a working, clear microphone.
- No voice changers, soundboards, or playing music over your mic.
- Speak English in main RP scenarios. Other languages are fine for designed character concepts.

## 5. Streaming & Content Creation

- You're welcome to stream and record. Make sure your overlay doesn't dox other players.
- Don't use information from someone else's stream IC ([Stream-sniping](rp-rules.md) is metagaming).
- Tag clips with **#AscendantRP** so we can find them.

## 6. Account Sharing

- One Cfx.re account per person. Don't let friends play on your character.
- Both accounts get banned if we catch shared play.

## 7. Real-Money Trading

- No selling characters, items, money, or accounts for real money.
- Donations through our [official store]([STORE_URL]) only.

## 8. Common Sense

- If you have to ask "is this against the rules?" — it probably is. Ask staff first.
