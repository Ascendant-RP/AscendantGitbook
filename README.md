## Why Ascendant?

- **Whitelisted community** — everyone in the city passed an application. No random griefers.
- **Player-driven economy** — money is earned. Crime, business, and legal jobs all interconnect in a closed economy.
- **Custom scripts & MLOs** — built in-house where it counts, not stitched from generic packs.
- **Active staff** — tickets get answered, reports get handled, and staff are held to the same standards as players.
- **Story-first design** — every gameplay decision is made to serve the roleplay, not the other way around.
