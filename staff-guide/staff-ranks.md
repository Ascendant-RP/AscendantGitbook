# Staff Ranks

| Rank | Role |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Final say. Owns the project. |
| **Co-Owner** | Owner-equivalent for day-to-day decisions. |
| **Head Admin** | Manages the admin team, handles escalations, runs internal reviews. |
| **Senior Admin** | Experienced admin, can mentor and approve tier 5 bans. |
| **Admin** | Full ticket and ban authority up to tier 4. |
| **Trial Admin** | Full ticket access, kick authority, must escalate bans. |
| **Moderator** | Discord + in-game ticket triage, kick authority. |
| **Trial Mod** | Discord triage, requires sign-off for in-game actions. |
| **Support** | Tech support and new-player questions only. |

## Promotion Path

Promotions happen during monthly review cycles. We look at:
- **Ticket volume + quality** (resolution time, professionalism, accuracy)
- **Community feedback** (pos and neg)
- **Initiative** — projects, suggestions, mentoring
- **Time at current rank** (typical minimum: 60 days)

There is **no entitlement** to promotion. You can be at one rank for a long time and still be a great staff member.

## Demotion / Removal

Causes:
- Repeated rule breaks (held to higher standard than players)
- Abuse of admin tools
- Inactivity (>14 days no contact, no notice)
- Toxicity toward staff or community
- Confidentiality breaches

Demotions are reviewed by Head Admin + an Owner. Decisions are final after one appeal.

## Department Structure

Some staff specialize:
- **Faction Liaisons** — assigned to specific gangs/factions
- **Whitelist Reviewers** — process applications
- **Dev Liaisons** — bridge between staff and development team

Specializations are additional, not in place of normal staff duties.
