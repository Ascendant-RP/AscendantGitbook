# 🏅 Staff Ranks

---

## Rank Structure

| Rank | Authority |
|---|---|
| 👑 **Owner** | Final say on all decisions. Owns the project. |
| 🔱 **Co-Owner** | Owner-equivalent for day-to-day decisions and escalations. |
| ⭐ **Head Admin** | Manages the admin team, handles escalations, runs internal reviews. |
| 🔵 **Senior Admin** | Experienced admin, can mentor staff and approve tier 5 bans. |
| 🟣 **Admin** | Full ticket and ban authority up to tier 4. |
| 🟡 **Trial Admin** | Full ticket access, kick authority — must escalate bans to Admin+. |
| 🟢 **Moderator** | Discord + in-game ticket triage, kick authority. |
| ⚪ **Trial Moderator** | Discord triage — requires sign-off for in-game actions. |
| 🔘 **Support** | Tech support and new-player guidance only. |

---

## Promotion

Promotions happen during **monthly review cycles**. We evaluate:

- **Ticket volume and quality** — resolution time, accuracy, professionalism
- **Community feedback** — both positive and negative signals
- **Initiative** — projects, suggestions, mentoring junior staff
- **Time at current rank** — typical minimum is 60 days per tier

{% hint style="warning" %}
There is **no entitlement to promotion**. You can hold one rank for a long time and still be a valued, excellent staff member. Promotion isn't the goal — doing good work is.
{% endhint %}

---

## Demotion & Removal

Causes for demotion or removal include:

- Repeated rule violations (held to a higher standard than players)
- Abuse of admin tools
- Inactivity — more than **14 days** with no contact or notice
- Toxicity toward the community or fellow staff
- Confidentiality breaches — leaking staff discussions
- Misuse of the punishment system

Demotion decisions are reviewed by **Head Admin + an Owner**. There is one appeal after which the decision is final.

## Specializations

Some staff take on additional focus areas — these are supplementary roles, not replacements for normal duties:

| Role | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Faction Liaison** | Assigned to specific gangs/factions to manage conflict and communication |
| **Whitelist Reviewer** | Processes and scores whitelist applications |
| **Dev Liaison** | Bridge between the staff team and development — communicates needs and feedback |
