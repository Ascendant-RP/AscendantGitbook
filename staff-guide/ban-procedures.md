# Ban & Kick Procedures

## Before You Ban

1. **Have evidence.** Logs, clips, screenshots — something that survives without your memory.
2. **Check the rule.** Cite the specific rule in the [Server Rules](../server-rules/README.md).
3. **Check history.** Use the lookup tool — escalate based on past warnings.
4. **Match the tier.** Reference the [Punishment System](../server-rules/punishments.md).

## Kick (Tier 3)

Used for:
- Removing a player from a heated situation
- Stopping continued rule-breaking
- Forcing a reconnect when bugs require it

Always kick **with a reason in the kick command**. The player sees this on screen. Be clear and not snarky.

## Temp Ban (Tier 4)

Used for clear-cut, single-incident violations:
- RDM with clear evidence
- VDM
- NLR break
- Metagaming
- Combat logging

Default durations:
- 1st offense: 1–3 days
- 2nd offense: 5–7 days
- 3rd offense: 14 days, escalate review

**Required:** ticket log entry with evidence link, rule cited, and your reasoning.

## Long Ban (Tier 5)

Requires **a second staff sign-off** (Senior Admin or higher).

Used for:
- Repeat tier 4 offenses
- Mass RDM (multiple kills, no RP)
- Bug exploitation for advantage
- Severe FailRP / griefing patterns

Default: 14–90 days based on severity.

## Permanent Ban (Tier 6)

Requires **Head Admin or Owner approval**, plus a second staff sign-off.

Used for:
- Cheating / mod menus / injectors
- Hate speech / doxxing / threats
- Real-money trading
- Ban evasion
- Repeated tier 5 offenses

## Ban Format

When issuing any ban, the log must include:

```
PLAYER: [name + cfx ID]
RULE: [specific rule cited]
EVIDENCE: [link to clip/screenshot/log]
DURATION: [days]
ISSUED BY: [you]
SIGNED OFF BY: [if required]
NOTES: [context]
```

Bans without proper logs are reverted on audit, and the issuing staff member is reviewed.

## Mistake Bans

If you ban someone in error:
1. Reverse it immediately.
2. Apologize to the player via ticket.
3. Tell the Head Admin.
4. Document the mistake.

Honest mistakes are not punished. Hiding them is.
