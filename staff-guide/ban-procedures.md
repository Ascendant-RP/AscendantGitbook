# 🚫 Ban & Kick Procedures

---

## Before Any Ban

{% hint style="warning" %}
Always complete these steps before issuing a ban. Bans without proper documentation are reverted on audit.
{% endhint %}

1. **Have evidence** — logs, video clips, screenshots. Something that stands without your memory.
2. **Cite the rule** — identify the specific rule from [Server Rules](../server-rules/README.md) that was broken.
3. **Check history** — use the player lookup tool. Escalate based on their existing record.
4. **Match the tier** — reference the [Punishment System](../server-rules/punishments.md) to apply the right duration.

---

## Kick (Tier 3)

Use when:
- Removing a player from a heated active situation
- Stopping continued rule-breaking that isn't ban-worthy yet
- Forcing a reconnect due to a bug or desync

{% hint style="info" %}
Always kick with a **clear reason** in the command. The player sees this on screen. Be professional — not snarky or vague.
{% endhint %}

---

## Temp Ban (Tier 4)

For clear-cut, evidence-backed single-incident violations:

- RDM with video proof
- VDM
- NLR break
- Metagaming
- Combat logging

**Default durations:**

| Offense Count | Duration |
|---|---|
| 1st offense | 1–3 days |
| 2nd offense | 5–7 days |
| 3rd offense | 14 days + escalation review |

**Required:** ticket log entry with evidence link, rule cited, and written reasoning.

---

## Long Ban (Tier 5)

Requires a **second staff sign-off** from Senior Admin or higher.

Used for:
- Repeat tier 4 offenses
- Mass RDM (multiple kills with no RP)
- Bug exploitation for personal gain
- Sustained FailRP or griefing patterns

**Default range:** 14–90 days based on severity and history.

---

## Permanent Ban (Tier 6)

Requires **Head Admin or Owner approval** plus a second staff sign-off.

Used for:
- Cheating — mod menus, injectors, third-party tools
- Hate speech, doxxing, or real-world threats
- Real-money trading (RMT)
- Ban evasion
- Repeated tier 5 violations

---

## Ban Log Format

Every ban must be logged using this format:

```
PLAYER:      [Character name + Cfx.re ID]
RULE:        [Specific rule violated — cite the section]
EVIDENCE:    [Link to clip / screenshot / log]
DURATION:    [Days or "Permanent"]
ISSUED BY:   [Your Discord tag]
SIGNED BY:   [Second staff member if required]
NOTES:       [Context, prior warnings, anything relevant]
```

{% hint style="danger" %}
Bans without a properly completed log entry are reverted during the next audit. The issuing staff member is then reviewed for procedure violations.
{% endhint %}

---

## Mistake Bans

If you ban someone in error:

1. **Reverse the ban immediately.**
2. Apologize to the player via ticket.
3. Notify the Head Admin promptly.
4. Document the error in the staff log.

Honest mistakes, reported promptly, are not punished. Hiding or ignoring them is.
