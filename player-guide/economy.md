# 💰 Economy & Money

Ascendant runs a **closed economy**. Every dollar in your pocket came from gameplay, another player, or a balanced job system — nothing is spawned in or given away.

---

## Money Types

| Type | Description | How to Access |
|---|---|---|
| 💵 **Cash** | Physical money on you. Can be lost if you're robbed or die. | Press `Z` to check your cash on hand |
| 🏦 **Bank** | Safe in your account. Protected from robbery. | ATMs or Maze Bank branches |
| 🖤 **Black Money** | Illegal, untraceable cash. Must be laundered before depositing. | Criminal activities |
| 📱 **Crypto** | Player-tradeable digital currency. Value fluctuates. | Phone → Crypto app |

---

## Laundering Black Money

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Walking into a bank with black money will flag you** — and potentially attract Special Investigations unit attention.
{% endhint %}

Black money can't be deposited at a bank directly. Clean it through:

- **Casino chips** — buy in with black money (at a markdown), cash out clean
- **Off-the-books businesses** — bars, clubs, and shops can process it with the right contacts
- **Player-to-player crypto trades** — convert and send

---

## Taxes

The government (controlled IC by the Mayor's office) takes a cut of:

| Transaction | Tax Rate |
|---|---|
| Job paychecks | 15–30% (based on income bracket) |
| Property purchases | 5% |
| Business sales | 10% |

Tax rates are set in character by the Mayor's office and can change through IC political decisions.

---

## Bills & Upkeep

Your character has recurring costs. Make sure your bank can cover them or you'll start receiving notices:

- 🏠 **Property taxes** — weekly, auto-deducted from bank
- 🚗 **Vehicle storage fees** — monthly, per vehicle in garage
- 📱 **Phone plan** — monthly subscription

{% hint style="danger" %}
Ignore bills long enough and you can lose property. There's no grace period — manage your finances.
{% endhint %}

---

## Insurance

| Type | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Vehicle Insurance** | If your car is destroyed, claim a replacement at PDM. Pay a deductible (10% of value). |
| **Health Insurance** | Covers ambulance and hospital fees. Without it, EMS bills are painful. |

Purchase insurance when buying a vehicle or through your phone.

---

## Markets & Player Trading

- **Player Market** (phone app) — buy and sell items P2P with escrow protection
- **Vehicle Marketplace** — list and browse used player cars
- **Real Estate Listings** — player-listed properties

Prices are determined by **supply and demand**. There are no fixed values — smart players make money by understanding the market.

These are bannable offenses with no warnings:

- Duplicate items or money via bug exploits
