# Economy & Money

Ascendant runs a **closed economy**. Money in = money out. We don't print cash to give away — every dollar in your pocket came from another player or a balanced job.

## Money Types

| Type | Description | How to Access |
|---|---|---|
| **Cash** | Physical money on you. Can be lost on death/robbery. | Your pocket — `Z` to check |
| **Bank** | Safe in your account. | ATMs or Maze Bank |
| **Black Money** | Illegal, untraceable cash. Must be laundered. | Crime activities |
| **Crypto** | Player-tradeable digital currency. | Phone → Crypto app |

## Laundering

Black money can't be deposited at a bank. Wash it through:

- **Casino** chips (with a markdown)
- **Off-the-books businesses** (bars, clubs, shops)
- **Player-to-player** crypto trades

Walking into Maze Bank with a duffel bag of black money will get you flagged — and possibly visited by Special Investigations.

## Taxes

The government takes a cut of:
- **Paychecks** (15–30% based on income bracket)
- **Property purchases** (5%)
- **Business sales** (10%)

Tax rates are set IC by the Mayor's office and can change.

## Insurance

- **Vehicle insurance** — pay a small premium when buying a car. If it's destroyed, you can claim a new one at PDM (with deductible).
- **Health insurance** — covers ambulance + hospital fees. Without it, EMS bills hurt.

## Bills & Upkeep

- **Property taxes** — weekly, paid automatically from bank
- **Vehicle storage fees** — monthly per vehicle in garage
- **Phone plans** — monthly subscription

If your bank can't cover bills, you'll get notices. Ignore them too long and you can lose property.

## Markets & Trading

- **Player Market** in your phone — buy/sell items P2P with escrow.
- **Vehicle Marketplace** for used cars.
- **Real Estate Listings** for housing.

Prices float based on supply and demand — no fixed values.

## Don't Try To...

- Duplicate items via bug exploits (bannable)
- Sell characters/items for real money (bannable)
- Coordinate price-fixing with other players for the sole purpose of crashing the market
