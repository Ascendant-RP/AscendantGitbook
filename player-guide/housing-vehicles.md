# Housing & Vehicles

## Housing

### Buying a Home
1. Open your phone → **Realty** app.
2. Browse listings or visit a Real Estate Agent IC.
3. Make an offer or pay listed price.
4. The home is now yours — keys can be shared with friends/family.

### Apartments
Cheaper starter option. Furnished, located in the city, and shared building access.

### Houses
Higher tier — bigger interior, garage, more storage. Requires more upfront capital and weekly upkeep.

### MLOs / Custom Properties
Some properties are custom interiors. These come up rarely on the market and tend to sell fast.

### Furniture & Decoration
Use the **Furniture** app to place items in your owned property. Save layouts and update anytime.

### Property Storage
Each property has a stash. Capacity scales with property tier. **Property storage is not insured** — break-ins can clean it out.

### Selling
List your property in the Realty app for any price. Other players can make offers. The city takes a 5% transaction tax.

## Vehicles

### Buying
- **PDM (Premium Deluxe Motorsport)** — civilian sedans, SUVs, trucks.
- **Auto Exotic** — sports and supercars (mid-tier).
- **Luxury Auto** — high-end exotics (require capital + sometimes IC clearance).
- **Used Car Lot** — player-resold vehicles, usually cheaper.

### Garages & Storage
- Personal garages spawn vehicles registered to your character.
- Use the **Garage** marker at any garage to access your vehicles.
- Vehicles left in the impound have a recovery fee.

### Modding
- **Bennys** for tuners and street builds.
- **LSC** for general modifications.
- Custom shops (player-owned) for unique IC builds.

### Insurance
Buy insurance at PDM when purchasing. If your car is destroyed and you have insurance:
- Wait the cooldown (15 min)
- Visit PDM and click **Insurance Claim**
- Pay deductible (10% of vehicle value)

Without insurance, destroyed = gone.

### Plates
Each vehicle has a unique plate. PD can run plates to find the registered owner. **Don't commit crime in your personal vehicle** unless you want a knock on the door.
