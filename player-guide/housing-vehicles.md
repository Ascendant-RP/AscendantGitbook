# 🏠 Housing & Vehicles

---

## Housing

### Buying a Home

1. Open your **phone → Realty app**.
2. Browse available listings or find a Real Estate Agent in character.
3. Make an offer or pay the listed price.
4. The property is now yours — you can share keys with trusted friends or family characters.

### Property Tiers

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Apartment** | Affordable starter option. Furnished, city-located, shared building access. |
| **House** | More space, personal garage, larger storage. Requires more capital + weekly upkeep. |
| **Custom MLO Property** | Unique interiors not found elsewhere. Rare on the market — they move fast. |

### Property Features

- **Furniture App** — place and arrange items in any owned property. Save layouts and update anytime.
- **Property Storage** — every property has a stash. Capacity scales with property tier.
- **Keys** — shareable with other players for trusted access.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Property storage is not insured.** A break-in can wipe it. Don't store everything you own in one place.
{% endhint %}

### Selling Property

List your property in the Realty app at any price. Other players can browse and make offers. The city takes a **5% transaction tax** on all sales.

## Vehicles

### Where to Buy

| Dealership | What You'll Find |
|---|---|
| **PDM** (Premium Deluxe Motorsport) | Civilian sedans, SUVs, trucks |
| **Auto Exotic** | Sports cars and mid-tier performance vehicles |
| **Luxury Auto** | High-end exotics — require significant capital |
| **Used Car Lot** | Player-resold vehicles, often cheaper than retail |

### Garages & Storage

- Personal garages store vehicles registered to your character.
- Access via the **Garage** marker at any garage location.
- Vehicles left in the impound cost a **recovery fee** to retrieve.

### Modifying Your Vehicle

| Shop | Speciality |
|---|---|
| **Benny's** | Tuner and street builds, liveries, custom looks |
| **LSC** | General modifications, performance upgrades |
| **Player-owned shops** | Unique IC builds, custom pricing |

### Vehicle Insurance

{% hint style="danger" %}
**No insurance = if it's destroyed, it's gone permanently.** Always insure your vehicles at purchase.
{% endhint %}

If you have insurance and your car is destroyed:

1. Wait 15 minutes (cooldown).
2. Visit PDM and click **Insurance Claim**.
3. Pay the deductible (10% of the vehicle's value).
4. Drive away.

### Plates & Avoiding Heat

Each vehicle has a unique registered plate. Law enforcement can run plates to find the registered owner.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Don't commit crime in your personal, registered vehicle unless you want a knock on your door later.
{% endhint %}
