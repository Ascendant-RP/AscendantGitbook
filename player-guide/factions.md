# Factions & Gangs

Organized groups — both legal and criminal — drive the city's biggest stories. Here's how to join one or start your own.

## Existing Factions

### Legal
- **LSPD** — Los Santos Police Department
- **BCSO** — Blaine County Sheriff's Office
- **EMS / Pillbox Medical**
- **DOJ** — Department of Justice
- **Government / Mayor's Office**

### Criminal (IC Only — Not Listed)
We don't publish active gang rosters. Find them IC.

## Joining a Faction

### Legal
Apply in `#government-careers` on [Discord]([DISCORD_INVITE_URL]). Each department has its own application, training academy, and probation period.

### Criminal
You don't "apply" to a gang. You meet them IC, build trust over time, and they patch you in. If you join Discord asking "how do I join a gang," you're doing it wrong.

## Starting a New Gang

To get **official faction status** (which unlocks gang storage, turf, and conflict approval):

1. Develop the gang **IC for at least 30 days** with a stable core of 4+ members.
2. Submit a Faction Application in `#faction-apps` including:
   - Gang name, colors, hierarchy
   - Backstory and territory
   - Member roster (Discord + character names)
   - 2–3 RP clips showcasing the gang
3. Wait for staff review (usually 1–2 weeks).
4. If approved, you'll be assigned a Faction Liaison from the staff team.

## Gang Conflict & Wars

- **Casual conflict** (street-level beef) doesn't need approval.
- **Wars** (sustained, lethal, multi-day campaigns) require:
  - Both gangs agreeing OOC
  - A war plan submitted in ticket
  - Staff approval and oversight
  - Defined start/end conditions and CK rules

See [Combat Rules](../server-rules/combat-rules.md) for the limits on shootouts, hostages, and NLR.
