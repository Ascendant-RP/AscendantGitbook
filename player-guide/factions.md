# 🤝 Factions & Gangs

Organized groups — both legal and criminal — drive the city's biggest stories. Here's how to join one or build your own from the ground up.

## Joining a Faction

### Legal Factions
Apply through `#government-careers` on [Discord](https://discord.gg/ascendantrp). Each department has its own application, training academy, and probationary period before you're a full member.

### Criminal Factions
You don't "apply" to a gang. You find them in character, build trust over real RP time, and they decide whether to bring you in. If your first move is DMing someone "how do I join a gang" — you're doing it wrong.

---

## Starting a New Gang

To earn **official faction status** (which unlocks gang turf, shared storage, and conflict approval):

1. Develop the gang **in character for at least 30 days** with a stable core of 4 or more members.
2. Submit a Faction Application in `#faction-apps` including:
   - Gang name, colors, hierarchy structure
   - IC backstory and territory
   - Member roster (Discord tags + character names)
   - 2–3 RP clips demonstrating the group's activity
3. Wait for staff review — typically **1–2 weeks**.
4. If approved, you'll be assigned a **Faction Liaison** from the staff team.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Applications without RP clip evidence are automatically denied. Show us the work, not just the concept.
{% endhint %}

## Gang Conflict & Wars

| Type | Requirements |
|---|---|
| **Casual conflict** (street-level beef, territory disputes) | No approval needed — just good RP |
| **Declared wars** (sustained, multi-day, lethal campaigns) | OOC agreement from both sides + staff approval + defined end conditions |

Wars require:
- Both gang leaders agreeing OOC
- A war plan submitted via ticket
- Staff approval and assigned oversight
- Clear start/end conditions and CK rules agreed upon

See [Combat Rules](../server-rules/combat-rules.md) for the limits on shootouts, hostages, and NLR during conflict.

---

## Faction Size Cap

To prevent any single group from dominating the server, official factions are capped at **25 active members**. Recruit selectively — quality over quantity.
