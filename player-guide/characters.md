# 👤 Creating a Character

Your character is your story. Make someone you actually want to play for **months**, not days.

---

## Character Slots

Each whitelisted account starts with **2 character slots**. Additional slots can be unlocked through [VIP tiers](../community/donations.md).

---

## The Creation Screen

When you first join, you'll see the character selection menu. Click **Create Character** and go through:

1. **Name & Date of Birth** — must be realistic. No stage names, no celebrity names.
2. **Gender & Nationality**
3. **Appearance** — full face, hair, body, and clothing customization.
4. **Backstory** *(optional but strongly encouraged)* — a short paragraph for your own reference.

{% hint style="info" %}
You can edit your appearance later at any **clothing store** or **barber shop** in the city.
{% endhint %}

---

## Writing a Good Backstory

Good backstories aren't about power — they're about **hooks** that create RP.

| ✅ Works Well | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|
| A retired gardener with gambling debts trying to go straight | An ex-Navy-SEAL billionaire with 12 black belts |
| A single parent who moved to LS for a fresh start | A character with no flaws, fears, or vulnerabilities |
| A mechanic who owes money to the wrong people | A character whose backstory is just a list of accomplishments |

**Give them a flaw.** Greedy, cowardly, addicted, naive, impulsive — something that creates drama and RP naturally.

---

## Starting Items

Every new character spawns with:

- **$500** cash on hand
- **$2,500** in the bank
- A phone
- ID and driver's license
- Basic clothing

---

## Character Death (CK — Character Kill)

A **CK** is the permanent death of a character. This is not the same as dying and going to the hospital (NLR). CKs only happen through:

{% hint style="danger" %}
CKs are **permanent and irreversible**. The character, their assets, and their relationships are gone. Think carefully before agreeing to one.
{% endhint %}

- **Player consent** — you sign off via ticket
- **Approved gang/faction conflict outcomes** — see [Factions](factions.md)
- **Staff-imposed CK** for severe rule violations

> Getting shot in a random street fight is **NLR**, not a CK. NLR = go to hospital, forget the scene.

---

## Switching Characters

Use `/logout` from your phone or the pause menu → **Quit to Character Select**.
