# Creating a Character

Your character is your story. Make them someone you actually want to play for **months**, not days.

## Character Slots

Each whitelisted account gets **[CHARACTER_SLOT_COUNT]** character slots. Additional slots can be unlocked through donation tiers (see [Donations](../community/donations.md)).

## The Creation Screen

When you join, you'll see the character selection menu. Click **Create Character** and you'll be taken through:

1. **Name & Date of Birth** — must be realistic. No nicknames, no celebrities.
2. **Gender & Nationality**
3. **Appearance** — full face, hair, body, clothing customization.
4. **Backstory** (optional but encouraged) — short paragraph for your records.

You can edit your appearance later at any **clothing store** or **barber**.

## Backstory Tips

- Keep it grounded. A retired gardener with gambling debts is more interesting than an ex-Navy-SEAL-billionaire.
- Give them a **flaw**. Greedy, cowardly, addicted, naive — something that creates RP.
- Don't write your character as immune to consequences. The good RP comes from losing.

- A phone
- ID and driver's license
- Basic clothing

## Character Death (CK — Character Kill)

A **CK** is permanent character death. CKs only happen by:

- **Player consent** (you sign off on it via ticket)
- **Approved gang/faction conflict outcomes** (see [Factions](factions.md))
- **Staff-imposed CK** for severe rule violations

You **don't** lose your character because some random shot you in the head. That's NLR — not CK.

## Switching Characters

Use `/logout` from your phone or the pause menu to return to character select.
