# 🆘 Support

Stuck? Here's how to get help fast.

---

## Quick Routing

| I have a… | Where to go |
|---|---|
| Tech issue (can't connect, crash, install problem) | `#tech-support` on [Discord](https://discord.gg/ascendantrp) |
| Bug to report | `#bug-reports` |
| Rule question | `#support-tickets` |
| Player to report | In-game `/report` **or** `#support-tickets` |
| Compensation request (item/money lost to bug) | `/report` with video evidence |
| Ban to appeal | `#ban-appeals` |
| Donation or billing issue | `#support-tickets` — mark as **Billing** |

---

## Opening an In-Game Ticket

While connected to the server, type:

```
/report [brief description of your issue]
```

A staff member will respond as soon as one is available. **Don't spam the command** — it pings all online staff each time.

---

## Opening a Discord Ticket

1. Go to `#support-tickets` in [Discord](https://discord.gg/ascendantrp).
2. Click the button for the appropriate category.
3. Fill out the prompts as completely as you can.
4. Wait — a staff member will respond in the thread.

---

## What to Include in Your Ticket

The more detail you provide upfront, the faster we can resolve your issue:

- ✅ Your **character name** and **Cfx.re ID**
- ✅ **What happened** — timeline and who was involved
- ✅ **Evidence** — clips, screenshots, or video links (Streamable, Imgur, etc.)
- ✅ **What you've already tried** (for technical issues)

---

## Response Times

| Ticket Type | Typical Response Time |
|---|---|
| In-game `/report` | Under 15 minutes during peak hours |
| Discord support ticket | Under 6 hours |
| Bug report | Triaged daily |
| Ban appeal | 5–7 days |
| Whitelist application | Up to 48 hours |

---

## What Support Does Not Cover

{% hint style="warning" %}
Opening a ticket for these will result in it being closed without response.
{% endhint %}

- "Can I have free money / a car / a weapon?" — No.
- "I lost something doing something I wasn't supposed to do" — No comp for self-inflicted losses.
- "I don't like this rule, change it" — Submit a suggestion in `#suggestions` instead.
- General RP complaints with no evidence or rule violation — those are RP outcomes, not support issues.

---

## Emergency Contact

If something serious is actively happening — a targeted harassment campaign, doxxing, or real threats:

{% hint style="danger" %}
Ping `@Staff` directly in `#support-tickets`. **Only use this for genuine emergencies.** Abuse of the emergency ping will result in a mute.
{% endhint %}
