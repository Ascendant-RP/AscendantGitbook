# About the Server

## Who We Are

Ascendant RP is a serious roleplay FiveM community committed to creating a long-term, immersive Los Santos experience. We launched on **[LAUNCH_DATE]** with the goal of building a server where character development, economy, and player-driven stories actually matter.

## What Makes Us Different

- **Built on QBox** — a modern, performance-focused fork of QBCore with active development and stability improvements.
- **Whitelisted** — every player goes through an application so we can keep the community high-quality.
- **Player-driven economy** — money is earned, not given. Crime, business, and legal jobs all interconnect.
- **Active staff** — tickets get answered, reports get handled, and staff are held to the same standards as players.
- **Custom scripts & MLOs** — we build our own systems where it makes sense, instead of stitching together generic packs.

1. **Roleplay first** — gameplay decisions should serve the story, not the other way around.
2. **Respect** — IC conflict is fine, OOC toxicity is not.
3. **Fair play** — no exploits, no metagaming, no unearned advantages.
4. **Community** — we win together.
