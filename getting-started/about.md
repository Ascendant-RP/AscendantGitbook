# 📖 About the Server

## Who We Are

Ascendant RP is a serious roleplay FiveM community committed to creating a long-term, immersive Los Santos experience. Our goal is a server where character development, economy, and player-driven stories actually matter — not just another GTA deathmatch with a /me here and there.

---

## What Makes Us Different

| Feature | What it Means |
|---|---|
| **QBox Framework** | Modern, performance-focused fork of QBCore — stable, actively developed, and built for heavy RP. |
| **Whitelisted** | Every player goes through an application. Higher baseline quality in every scene. |
| **Closed Economy** | Money is earned, not spawned. Crime, business, and legal jobs all interconnect. |
| **Custom Scripts** | We build our own systems where generic packs fall short. |
| **Custom MLOs** | Unique interiors you won't find on other servers. |
| **Active Staff** | Tickets are answered. Reports are handled. Staff are held to the same standard as players. |

## Our Values

1. **Roleplay first** — gameplay decisions should serve the story, not the other way around.
2. **Respect** — IC conflict is encouraged. OOC toxicity is not tolerated.
3. **Fair play** — no exploits, no metagaming, no unearned advantages.
4. **Community** — we win and grow together.
