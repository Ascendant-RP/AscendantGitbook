# 📋 Whitelist Application

Ascendant is a **whitelisted server**. Every player must apply and be approved before they can connect.

---

## Why Do We Whitelist?

Whitelisting protects the community. It filters out griefers, sets clear expectations, and ensures everyone in the server has at least read the rules and can write a coherent character concept.

---

## Requirements

{% hint style="warning" %}
Make sure you meet **all** of these before applying. Applications missing requirements are auto-denied.
{% endhint %}

- **Age:** 16 or older
- **Discord** account, linked to your Cfx.re/FiveM profile
- **Working microphone** — no mic, no RP
- Read and understood the [Server Rules](../server-rules/README.md)

---

## How to Apply

1. Join our Discord: [discord.gg/ascendantrp](https://discord.gg/ascendantrp)
2. Verify your account in `#verify`.
3. Head to `#whitelist-application` and click the **Apply** button.
4. Fill out the application — takes around 10–15 minutes.
5. Wait for a staff review. **Most applications are reviewed within 48 hours.**

---

## What We Look For

| Criteria | What Good Looks Like |
|---|---|
| **Character Backstory** | Clear, grounded, believable — not a superhero or meme character |
| **RP Knowledge** | Understands RDM, VDM, Metagaming, Powergaming, NLR, FailRP |
| **Character Name** | Realistic (no "John Wick", "Big Smoke", "xXDarkness420Xx") |
| **Writing Tone** | Mature, effort shown — even if your English isn't perfect |

---

## If You're Denied

{% hint style="danger" %}
Do **not** evade a whitelist denial by creating a new account. This results in a permanent ban on all linked accounts.
{% endhint %}

- You'll receive written feedback in your Discord DMs.
- You may reapply after **7 days**, taking the feedback into account.
- Repeated low-effort applications may result in a permanent denial.
