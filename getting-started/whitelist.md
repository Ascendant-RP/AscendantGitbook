# Whitelist Application

Ascendant is a whitelisted server. Every player must apply and be approved before they can connect.

## Why Whitelist?

Whitelisting protects the community. It filters out trolls, sets clear expectations, and ensures everyone in the server has at least read the rules.

## Requirements

- **Age:** 16+
- **Discord** account, linked to FiveM/Cfx.re
- **Working microphone**
- Read and agree to the [Server Rules](../server-rules/README.md)

## How to Apply

1. Join our [Discord]([DISCORD_INVITE_URL]).
2. Verify your account in `#verify`.
3. Go to `#whitelist-application` and click the apply button.
4. Fill out the form (takes ~10–15 minutes).
5. Wait for a staff member to review — most apps are reviewed within **48 hours**.

## What We Look For

- Clear, well-thought-out character backstory
- Understanding of basic RP terms (RDM, VDM, Metagaming, Powergaming, NLR)
- Realistic character concept (no Batman, no anime protagonists)
- Maturity in writing tone

## If You're Denied

You'll receive feedback in your Discord DMs. You can reapply after **7 days**, taking the feedback into account. Repeat low-effort applications may result in a permanent denial.

## Already Approved?

Head over to [How to Connect](how-to-connect.md) and we'll see you in the city.
