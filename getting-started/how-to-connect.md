# How to Connect

Once your whitelist application is approved, you can join the server.

## Method 1: Direct Connect (Fastest)

1. Open FiveM.
2. Press **F8** to open the console.
3. Type:
   ```
   connect [SERVER_CONNECT_URL]
   ```
4. Press Enter and wait for assets to download (first connect can take 5–15 minutes).

## Method 2: Server Browser

1. Open FiveM.
2. Click **Play** → **Servers**.
3. Search for **Ascendant RP**.
4. Click the server and hit **Connect**.

## Method 3: Cfx.re Link

Click [https://cfx.re/join/[CFX_CODE]](https://cfx.re/join/[CFX_CODE]) — FiveM will open and connect automatically.

## First-Time Connect

The first time you join you'll download:
- Custom textures and models
- Map MLOs (interiors)
- Vehicle assets
- UI resources

This can take a while depending on your internet. **Don't disconnect mid-download** or you'll have to start over.

## Connection Issues

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| "You are not whitelisted" | Your application was denied or not yet approved — check Discord. |
| Stuck on "Awaiting Scripts" | Wait — large resource servers take 60s+ to fully load. |
| Disconnected with "Connection failed" | Verify your Discord is linked to your Cfx.re account. |
| Crash during loading screen | Clear FiveM cache and re-join. |

If you're stuck for more than 10 minutes, ask in the [Discord]([DISCORD_INVITE_URL]) tech-support channel.
