# 🚀 Getting Started

Welcome aboard! This section takes you from **"I just heard about Ascendant"** to **"I'm spawned in Los Santos and ready to RP."**

{% hint style="info" %}
**New here?** Follow the steps below in order. Skipping the whitelist step means you won't be able to connect.
{% endhint %}

---

## Steps to Join

1. **Install FiveM** — the launcher that powers custom multiplayer servers.
2. **Read the rules** — seriously, read them before applying.
3. **Submit a whitelist application** — join our Discord and apply.
4. **Connect to the server** — once approved, you're in.
5. **Create your character** and start your story.
